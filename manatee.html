Manatee

Learn about Manatees!

  • The manatee is a large marine mammal with an egg-shaped head, flippers and a flat tail.
  • Manatees are also known as sea cows.and can be eaten by other animals
  • manatees are more closely related to elephants
  • Manatees range in size from 8 to 13 feet (2.4 to 4 meters) and can weigh 440 to 1,300 lbs. (200 to 590 kilograms).
  • they can swim up to 15 mph (24 km/h) in short bursts when they feel a need for speed,
