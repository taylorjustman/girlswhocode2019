Snow Leopard Facts

Snow leopards are animals that live on the rocky mountains around Central Asia. They have very thick white coat and have wide feet that are fully covered to protect them from the snow. Their coat that is covered with black/bownspots that help them camoufaulge into their surrounding and hunt their prey.
